About Me

I am a graduate of the Revised Bachelor of Science in Industrial Technology major in Drafting Technology from the Catanduanes State University of Region V, Philippines. Other than this degree, I also have a National Certificate II in Technical Drafting.
I am particularly skilled in Revit and AutoCAD with regards to making building plans involving all trades. I am also highly skilled in SketchUp which I used during my internship where I modeled and made the shop drawings for various wood and metal furnishing in our projects.
Other than most CAD and BIM software, I am also capable of using other graphics software such as Photoshop, Lumion, & Blender which I use for presentations and renderings.
I am efficient and methodical in my works. I also have impeccable organizational and problem-solving skills. I am highly adaptive to any work environment and have always performed professionally in all settings.
